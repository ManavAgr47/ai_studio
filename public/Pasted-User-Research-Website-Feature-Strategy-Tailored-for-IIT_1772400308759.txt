User Research & Website Feature Strategy
Tailored for IIT AI Studio's Three Core AI Tools

Executive Summary
IIT AI Studio is developing a website to showcase three core AI automation services: Calling Agents, Newsletter Magazine, and Automatic Email Sender. The goal is to drive lead generation and direct sales by clearly addressing user problems and positioning each tool as a purpose-built solution. This document captures user personas, journey flows, messaging, and feature recommendations based on those three services.

User Personas
Raj (Sales Manager, SMB, Age 34)
Pain Point: His sales team spends hours daily making repetitive calls to cold leads.
Needs: An automated solution that can pitch products and update CRM records.
Tool Fit: Calling Agents
Meera (Startup Founder, Age 29)
Pain Point: Receives 10+ newsletters daily but has no time to read and summarize them for business use.
Needs: A single, digestible update tailored to her preferences.
Tool Fit: Newsletter Magazine
Ankit (Marketing Associate, Tech Firm, Age 26)
Pain Point: Manually drafts and sends cold emails for lead gen campaigns every day.
Needs: A tool that automates email generation, sending, and follow-up.
Tool Fit: Automatic Email Sender

6W2H Analysis
Who: Small business teams, startup founders, sales/marketing professionals.
What (Problem): Time-intensive manual workflows: cold calling, email outreach, newsletter digestion.
What (Our Service): 3 core tools:
Calling Agents (voice-based AI calling)
Newsletter Magazine (LLM-curated newsletter summaries)
Automatic Email Sender (context-based email automation)
Why (They Buy): Users want faster workflows, better efficiency, and scalable outreach with low effort.
Where (They Come From): LinkedIn, founder/tech newsletters, direct search (e.g., "AI email automation for startups").
When: During campaign planning, product launches, quarterly reviews.
How: Users land via a targeted ad or article, browse tool cards, and click into individual product pages.
How Much: Compared to hiring or using multiple SaaS tools, IIT AI Studio offers modular, lower-cost automation. ROI is high from the first successful deployment.

User Journey Flow
Scenario: Meera (Founder) discovers the "Newsletter Magazine" tool
Searches: “summarize newsletters with AI” → finds our site.
Lands on Homepage → Sees question-style headline: “Reading newsletters takes too long?”
Clicks into Newsletter Tool Page
Reads key benefits: LLM curation, sends summaries, replies enabled.
Sees sample screenshots + testimonial
Call to Action: “Try It on Your Inbox”
Clicks demo or contact form → Enters email
